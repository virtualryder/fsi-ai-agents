# User Guides

Per-persona documentation for the people who operate the agents day to day. Everyone starts with the fundamentals; your role guide builds on it.

| Read | If you are |
|---|---|
| [REVIEWER-FUNDAMENTALS.md](REVIEWER-FUNDAMENTALS.md) | **Everyone, first.** What the agents do and never do, how queues and decisions work, what your approval means, the standing rules. |
| [GUIDE-BSA-AML.md](GUIDE-BSA-AML.md) | AML analysts, AML supervisors, BSA officers (Agents 01 & 02 — alert triage, investigations, SAR approval) |
| [GUIDE-CREDIT-FAIR-LENDING.md](GUIDE-CREDIT-FAIR-LENDING.md) | Senior underwriters and fair-lending officers (Agent 08 — decision review, adverse action, fairness oversight) |
| [GUIDE-OPERATIONS.md](GUIDE-OPERATIONS.md) | Document intake ops, payments/dispute teams, sanctions officers, collections supervisors (Agents 09, 10, 12) |
| [GUIDE-AUDITOR.md](GUIDE-AUDITOR.md) | Internal audit and examination-support staff (read-only role; decision reconstruction and sampling programs) |

Personas not yet covered (KYC ops — 03, fraud ops — 04, RMs — 05, compliance analysts — 06, surveillance — 07, model validators — 11) follow the fundamentals plus their agent's `README` and `regulatory-compliance.md`; dedicated guides are written during pilot onboarding for the agents in scope. Operational procedures for leads and platform staff live in [`runbooks/`](../../runbooks/).
